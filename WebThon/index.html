NEET

NEET, short for the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test, is a highly competitive entrance examination in India. It serves as the gateway for admission to undergraduate medical and dental courses (MBBS/BDS) in government and private medical institutions across the country. NEET replaced several individual medical entrance exams and became mandatory for aspirants seeking admission to medical colleges.

UPSC

The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) is one of India's premier recruiting agencies responsible for conducting civil services examinations and various other recruitment tests for central government positions. Established under the Constitution of India, UPSC conducts exams like the Civil Services Examination (CSE), Engineering Services Examination (ESE), Combined Defence Services Examination (CDSE).

JEE

The Joint Entrance Examination is a prominent entrance examination in India for admission to undergraduate engineering programs at prestigious institutions such as the IITs, National Institutes of Technology (NITs), and other renowned engineering colleges. JEE is conducted in two phases: JEE Main and JEE Advanced. JEE Main serves as the preliminary exam, while JEE Advanced is the gateway to admission into the IITs.
